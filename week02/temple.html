Democratic Republic of Congo

The Democratic Republic of the Congo, located in Central Africa, is the second-largest country on the continent by area. It is known for its vast natural resources, the majestic Congo River, and the lush Congo Basin rainforest. The vibrant capital city, Kinshasa, is a major cultural and musical hub in Africa, blending diverse traditions with a bustling urban lifestyle.
